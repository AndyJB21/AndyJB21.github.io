My Favorite Animals

  1. Snow Leopard



    • About: They are about 24″ at the shoulders and are over 6 ft in length (plus 3 ft for tail length). They weigh up to 125 lbs and are located in central asia

    • Fun Fact: They live on Mountains and are found at altitudes of up to 17,000 ft.

  2. Koala


    • About: They reach between 2-3 ft at the shoulder and 4-6 ft long. An adult can weigh up to 250 lbs

    • Fun Fact: They have bumps on their paws that act like thumbs.

  3. Arctic Wolf



    • About: Shoulder height can be 25-31″ and their body length about 3-5 ft. An adult male weighs about 175 lbs. In captivity, they can live up to 17 years but only 7 in the wild. Their fur may have hints of red, white, grey or black.

    • Fun Fact: Because of their fur, it can endure years of sub-zero temperatures

  4. Giant Panda

    • About: They reach between 2-3 ft at the shoulder and 4-6 ft long. An adult panda can weigh up to 250 lbs

    • Fun Fact: They have bumps on their paws that act like thumbs.
